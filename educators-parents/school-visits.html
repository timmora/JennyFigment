Author Visits

Bring Jenny to Your School!

Visits are best suited for students in grades 1-5 and include a chapter reading, Q&A session, and interactive fairy tale fun! Best of all, visits are absolutely free (up to two grade-level visits; additional visits for a nominal charge) and include an opportunity for your students to pre-order books at a special student price (no minimum order necessary, but schools with a minimum of 25 orders will receive a complimentary series set for their library!).

The Presentation

What to Expect

Each school visit is tailored to your students' age and interests, but here's what every visit includes:

📖

Live Reading & Storytelling

Jennifer reads from the Jenny Figment books with energy, humor, and character voices that bring the story to life.

✨

Imagination Workshop

An interactive activity where students create their own characters and story ideas — sparking their inner author.

❓

Q&A & Book Signing

Students ask Jennifer anything about writing, characters, and her creative process. Signed copies available for purchase.

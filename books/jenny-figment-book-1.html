The Extraordinary Imagination of Jenny Figment

By Jennifer Hayes

Ages 7–11 Grades 1–5 IndieReader Approved ★★★★★
★★★★★

Step into the enchanting world of Jenny Figment, where imagination knows no bounds and adventures are anything but ordinary. She dreams up conversations with her faithful dog, Fudge, experiences the thrill of shrinking to the size of a baby spider and bouncing on a web-spun trampoline, and rides her noble steed across a magical countryside. When her town encounters a crisis, Jenny's imagination, along with a remarkable cast of characters, becomes the key to saving the day. With a group of clever, hard-working ghost friends like Jenny's, the impossible becomes possible! Ideal for early middle-grade readers aged 7-11, and anyone who revels in extraordinary tales filled with adventure, friendship, and the magic of the imagination.

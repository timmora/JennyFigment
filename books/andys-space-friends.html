Andy's Space Friends

By Jennifer Hayes

Ages 6–11Grades 1–5
★★★★★

Jenny and Andy have become best friends—going on space missions to Planet Sandbox, sending messages to Andy's space friends using their super-secret space communicator, and playing hide and seek with the shrinking swarbles that live under the Big Toy. When the school merry-go-round turns into a rocket ship on Halloween night, the two friends begin the adventure of a lifetime! Landing on Planet Olii, the planet of the space friends, readers follow the explorers through giant forests, across frozen landscapes, and over fields of gemstones to the end of the planet to find the dreaded Bundeva. Will they find him in time to save the planet? Book Two in the Jenny Figment series, "Andy's Space Friends," is a magical read (and perfect read aloud) for early middle grade adventure seekers, ages 6 - 11.

The Story of

Jennifer Hayes

"Use your imagination!"

Jennifer had a whimsical childhood during the 1970s and 80s, with imaginary friends and a trusty noble steed as her constant companions. As she grew older, her imagination and love for children’s books remained, guiding her to become a teacher. She’s now on an extraordinary adventure of her own into the world of children’s literature.

She is the author of The Extraordinary Imagination of Jenny Figment (January 5, 2024), Andy's Space Friends (September 2, 2024), and The Riddle of the Fairytale Village (October 19, 2025). Jennifer loves animals, especially pocket dragons, and has a passion for baking pinkerdoodles. She lives in Illinois with her husband, daughter, one cat, and two dogs—who sadly do not talk (though sometimes Jennifer imagines they do).
